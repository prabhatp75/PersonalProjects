Rules and Guidelines

For using the Shri Ram Shalaka Prashnavali

Spiritual Prerequisites

  • Purity: Approach the Shalaka after cleansing yourself (washing hands and face) and with a calm, clear mind.
  • Sincerity: Your question should be genuine and asked with full faith and devotion towards Lord Rama.
  • Focus: Find a quiet, clean space where you will not be disturbed. Concentrate solely on your question and pray to Lord Rama before making your selection.

How to Use

  • Meditate on your question: Although you don't need to type it, hold your question clearly in your mind.
  • Make a selection: With your eyes closed (or unfocused), move your cursor over the grid and click on any letter that you feel drawn to.
  • Receive the answer: A verse (chaupai) will be revealed, along with its meaning and conclusion. This is the divine guidance for your question.

Important Principles

  • This is a tool for seeking divine guidance, not for fortune-telling or making trivial decisions.
  • The guidance received should be interpreted with wisdom and in the context of righteous action (Dharma).
  • Have unwavering faith in the wisdom of Lord Rama, regardless of the answer you receive.
